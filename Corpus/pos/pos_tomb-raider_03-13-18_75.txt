TOMB RAIDER is a fastpaced fun action adventure film Its adaptation of and improvement on the excellent source material and display of many iconic game moments are a delight to see on screen and Alicia Vikanders performance captures the strongwilled and intelligent personality of Lara Croft perfectly Enhanced by an emotional throughline about the love between a father and daughter TOMB RAIDER rises above most of the films in this genre and proves that good video game adaptations can be made It left this fan relieved satisfied and wanting more
