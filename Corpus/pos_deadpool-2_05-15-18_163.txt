High on the coattails of the neverending superhero season delivered from the originals release in early 2016 Deadpool 2 completely and utterly ploughs to the front of the stage in gratuitous fashion This is a sequel that both delivers and subverts the expectations we had from the lightning in a bottle magic of Deadpool scene to scene it lampoons the concept of sequelitis and cuts straight to what can only be described as Foxsploitation
